Know about characters

Naruto Uzumaki

The main character and protagonist of the series. He is an orphan who has a dangerous fox-like entity sealed within his body. Naruto aspires to become the Hokage, or leader, of his village.

Sasuke Uchiha

Naruto's best friend and rival. Sasuke is defined by the actions of his brother Itachi and undergoes significant changes throughout the story.

Sakura Haruno

A member of Team 7. Sakura initially takes a backseat to Naruto and Sasuke but eventually becomes one of the best medical ninjas in the Hidden Leaf Village.

Itachi Uchiha

Sasuke's older brother. Itachi was a member of the Akatsuki and was considered a prodigy by the Uchiha due to his talents and intelligence.

Kakashi Hatake

The leader of Team 7. Kakashi was the most popular Naruto character based on the Character Poll 1 that came out with manga chapter 60.

Pain

Pain aka Nagato belongs to the Uzumaki clan and is one of the main villains in Naruto. Nagato has a lot of aliases in Naruto Shippuden, one of them being Pain as it is more of a concept than a person, “The Six Paths of Pain”.

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is a young boy from the Hidden Leaf Village and the biggest problem child at the ninja academy. He causes mischief every day and is constantly being scolded by his teacher, Iruka Umino. He has fallen so far behind in his studies that his graduation is uncertain, but Naruto actually dreams of becoming the leader of the village, known as the Hokage, and vows to one day become the No. 1 ninja.However, all the adults in the village treat Naruto with disdain due to a secret surrounding his birth. 12 years ago, when Naruto was just a baby, the Nine-Tailed Fox attacked the village, and the only way to stop it was to seal it inside of him. This fact is first revealed to him by Mizuki, an instructor at the academy who uses Naruto to steal the Scroll of Sealing from the current Hokage. Once the deed is done, he appears before the young ninja and discloses the shocking truth behind his birth. Naruto manages to defeat Mizuki with Iruka’s help, who despite knowing about Naruto’s past, has always believed in him. After learning Iruka’s true feelings, Naruto gains a renewed determination to pursue his dream.After successfully graduating from the academy alongside fellow students Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno, Naruto finally takes the first steps on his journey as a real ninja!

Sasuke uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is one of the last surviving members of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan. After his older brother, Itachi, slaughtered their clan, Sasuke made it his mission in life to avenge them by killing Itachi. He is added to Team 7 upon becoming a ninja, and, through competition with his rival and best friend, Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke starts developing his skills but eventually grows dissatisfied with his progress. He defects from Konoha so that he can acquire the strength needed to exact his revenge and master the Cursed Seal of Heaven. His years of seeking vengeance and the actions that followed became increasingly demanding, irrational, and isolated from others, leading him to be branded as an international criminal. After learning the truth of his brother's sacrifice, later proving instrumental in ending the Fourth Shinobi World War, and being happily redeemed by Naruto, Sasuke decides to return to Konoha and dedicate his life to help protect the village and its inhabitants, becoming referred to as the "Supporting Kage"

Sakura Haruno

Sakura is the only child of Kizashi and Mebuki Haruno. She had a healthy childhood, raised by her parents without any serious tragedy or complications, unlike her team members. When she entered Konoha's Academy, a few of the girls in her class started picking on her because of her broad forehead. Sakura tried to combat their teasing by hiding her forehead with her bangs, but this caused the girls to bully her even more. Ino Yamanaka, one of her classmates, saw this, and defended Sakura from her bullies and encouraged her to embrace her forehead rather than hide it.Over the following years, Ino's guidance and friendship helped Sakura become comfortable with herself and develop into her own person. Though she felt indebted to Ino for helping her, Sakura began to feel that she was living in Ino's shadow when she, instead, wanted to be Ino's equal.

Itachi Uchiha

Itachi first appeared in Volume 16 of the Naruto manga and anime in March 2003 and April 2004, respectively. The original manga ran from 1999–2014, with a follow-up volume in 2015. Versions of the anime series ran on TV from 2002–17.Itachi had black hair, tied into a ponytail with long parted bangs. He wore a headband with a leaf symbol with a horizontal slash over it, signifying his “missing-nin” status, meaning that he had cut ties with his native village, the Hidden Leaf Village. Itachi was also often outfitted in a long black robe with white and red cloud patterns on it, signaling his allegiance to the Akatsuki, a terrorist organization. He rarely showed emotion but was prone to shows of force. Having been traumatized by witnessing a previous war, he was a pacifist and would often run from confrontation. He possessed the powerful Sharingan and was a ninja prodigy in his youth.Itachi is the brother of one of the main characters of Naruto, Sasuke. For much of the series’s duration, Itachi was reviled by fans for killing his and Sasuke’s entire clan, the Uchiha clan. It’s eventually revealed that Itachi didn’t murder his clan in cold blood. In fact, he was ordered to murder them by the leadership of his village to stop a coup d’etat, meaning that he acted in his village’s and Sasuke’s best interests the whole time. This was done to prevent another world war between the villages, which Itachi was afraid of. After killing his clan, he was allowed to spare his brother Sasuke, hoping that the hatred and trauma from the event would mold him into a strong ninja. Afterwards, he became part of the Akatsuki as a spy, making Itachi more prominent as an antagonist.

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake is a character from the anime Naruto who is known as "Kakashi of the Sharingan" and "Copy Ninja". He is a shinobi from Konoha's Hatake clan and is considered one of Konoha's most talented ninja. Kakashi is skilled in ninjutsu, including the Multiple Shadow Clone Technique, and is also proficient with a variety of weapons, such as bows and arrows, kusarigama, chains, and wire strings.Kakashi is the son of Sakumo Hatake, one of the most powerful shinobi Konoha has ever produced, known as "White Fang of the Leaf". Kakashi is a child prodigy who is hailed as the best of his generation during his time at the ninja academy. He becomes a Genin at age 5, a Chuunin at age 6, and later a Jounin and a member of the AnBu Assassination squad for Hidden Leaf Village.Kakashi is serious and observant, and approaches fights with unwavering attentiveness. This seriousness can also lead him to come off as painfully blunt when his students require a stern lesson. He values teamwork and the lives of his students over all else, even if it jeopardizes their mission.Kakashi is often looked to for advice and leadership. He teaches the importance of teamwork to his students on team 7, a lesson he received from his childhood friend Obito Uchiha. After the fourth shinobi world war, Kakashi becomes Konoha's sixth hokage.

Pain

Pain, also known as Nagato, is a character in the anime series Naruto who is the leader of Akatsuki and Amegakure. Pain is a member of the Uzumaki clan, a powerful ninja clan, and has the ability to generate large amounts of chakra. His power comes from the Rinnegan, an eye technique that allows him to manipulate all six forms of nature.Pain, also known as Nagato, is a character in the anime series Naruto who is the leader of Akatsuki and Amegakure.Pain's goal is to capture the tailed beasts that are sealed in people, and he seals most of the beasts in a Gedo statue. His advisor then tells him to capture the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox, which is sealed inside Naruto Uzumaki, the series' protagonist.Pain has a tragic past. He was born on the outskirts of Amegakure to Ise and Fusō, and during the Second Shinobi World War, two shinobi from Konoha broke into Nagato's house and killed his parents. Pain became disillusioned with the world after experiencing the loss of his family and friends to war, and began to believe that only pain and suffering could bring about peace. He believes that no one can understand the value of peace without first experiencing the horror of war, and makes a point in teaching suffering to anyone he speaks to.Pain is partnered with Konan, and the three were trained by Jiraiya in Ninjutsu. Pain's main body is the Deva Path, which has gravitational-like control, which allows it to push things away and attract them.
